Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

At GLP Flow, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

What We Collect

We collect minimal data. The only information we collect is your email address, and only when you choose to subscribe to our newsletter.

How We Use Your Email

Your email is used solely to deliver the GLP Flow newsletter to you. We send weekly meal plans, recipes, and tips for people on GLP-1 medications. We never sell or share your email address for marketing or advertising purposes.

Third Parties

We use two trusted third-party services:

  • Beehiiv handles email delivery and newsletter management. When you subscribe, your email is stored with Beehiiv. See Beehiiv's privacy policy.
  • Cloudflare provides hosting and analytics for our website. This helps us understand traffic and improve performance. See Cloudflare's privacy policy.

Cookies

We use minimal cookies. Cloudflare may place cookies on your device for analytics and website optimization. These are not used for tracking or advertising. You can disable cookies in your browser settings if you prefer.

Your Rights

  • Unsubscribe anytime: Every newsletter includes an unsubscribe link. Click it, and you'll be removed immediately.
  • Request deletion: Email us at hello@glpflow.app and we'll delete your email address from our records within 5 business days.
  • No selling of data: We do not sell, share, or trade your information with anyone.

Data Security

Your email is stored securely by Beehiiv using industry-standard encryption. We only access it to manage your newsletter subscription.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we'll note the date at the top of this page. Continued use of GLP Flow means you accept any changes.

Questions?

Email us at hello@glpflow.app with any privacy questions or concerns.
