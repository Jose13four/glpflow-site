About GLP Flow

GLP Flow exists for one reason: to help people on GLP-1 medications eat well.

Whether you're on Ozempic, Wegovy, Mounjaro, or Zepbound, the first weeks and months are confusing. Your appetite changes. Your stomach feels different. Traditional recipes don't work anymore. You're left figuring out how to eat in a way that actually feels good.

We believe in practical food planning

This isn't a medical site. We're not doctors. We're people who've watched friends and family navigate GLP-1 medications and realized there's a gap in what's available: honest, practical advice about food.

GLP Flow provides:

  • Meal plans designed for how your body actually feels on GLP-1 — not generic plans that assume a normal appetite
  • Protein-first recipes that are simple to make and actually sit well
  • Honest advice about what works, what doesn't, and why
  • Weekly guidance delivered to your inbox, so you don't have to figure it out alone

Made by people who care

Our team is passionate about making the GLP-1 journey easier. We've been there. We know the confusion. We know what works. And we're committed to building resources that actually help people feel better and eat with confidence.

Not medical advice

We want to be clear: GLP Flow is a food planning resource, not a medical resource. Always talk to your healthcare provider about your medication, side effects, and what's right for your body. We're here to help with the practical meal planning part.
